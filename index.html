What are Noms?

NOMs (Notion Operation Modules) are tiny tools (nom-nom) to improve your Notion workflow. Whether you are merging property options or cleaning up unused entries, NOMs aim to streamline your Notion experience.

  • Notion Link Enhancer

    Improve your Notion database by auto-adding favicons, image and title from your url property.

  • Delete Duplicate Entries

    Find and remove duplicate entries in your Notion database.

    Try It
  • Remove Unused Select/Multi-Select Options

    Clean your Notion database by removing unused options from select or multi-select properties.

    Try It
  • Merge Property Options

    Merge options in a Notion select or multi-select property.

  • Sort Property Options

    Sort the options of a select or multi-select property in your Notion database alphabetically.
